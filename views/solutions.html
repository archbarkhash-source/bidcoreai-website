About BidcoreAI

Your questions about the AI-intelligent end-to-end bidding platform — answered clearly.

BidcoreAI is an AI-intelligent end-to-end bidding solutions platform built for contractors, subcontractors, and preconstruction teams. It manages the entire bidding workflow — from drawing uploads and quantity takeoffs to CSI cost estimation, automated bid packages, subcontractor management, bid leveling, and AI-written proposals. Everything in one connected workspace, updated automatically every 15 days with new capabilities.
BidcoreAI is designed for General Contractors, Subcontractors, Estimators, Preconstruction Managers, Builders, Developers, and Construction Service Providers — especially teams that want to bid faster, reduce manual work, and improve win opportunities without adding headcount. Whether you're a solo estimator or a growing firm managing multiple projects simultaneously, the platform scales to your workflow.
BidcoreAI directly addresses the most common and costly bidding challenges: slow estimating workflows, manual quantity takeoffs consuming days of estimator time, repetitive admin tasks, disorganized bid files across email and spreadsheets, tight bid deadlines with too few resources, limited estimating capacity when bid volume spikes, inconsistent proposal preparation, and difficulty managing multiple projects at once.
It means BidcoreAI supports the entire bid journey inside one ecosystem — from the moment you receive project documents all the way to final submission. You receive documents, review plans, perform takeoffs in the Quantity Takeoff Workspace, build your cost estimate using your private CSI database, generate bid packages, send automated ITBs to subcontractors, level incoming sub bids, and produce AI-written proposals — without switching tools, copying data between spreadsheets, or relying on email chains.
AI is embedded throughout the platform — not just in one feature. It assists with quantity takeoff suggestions in the Takeoff Workspace, automatic scope extraction and risk flagging from uploaded documents, bid checklist generation, proposal drafting for federal and commercial projects (Cover Letter, Technical Approach, Management Plan, Price Schedule), real-time margin analysis in Bid Costing with risk alerts, and L2/L3 schedule generation aligned to your estimate. AI is designed to support professional judgment — not replace it.
BidcoreAI supports the full spectrum of US construction trades: Drywall & Framing, Lumber, Roofing & Siding, Flooring, Doors & Windows, Concrete, MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing), Painting, Interior Fit-Out, and General Trades. The CSI MasterFormat cost structure covers all 50 divisions, making it suitable for both specialty subcontractors and large general contractors managing multi-trade projects.
The platform includes: AI Document Analysis (scope extraction, risk flags, bid checklists on upload), Quantity Takeoff Workspace (built-in takeoff management), CSI Cost Database across 50 divisions (materials, labor, equipment, assemblies), Bid Workspace (AI-generated bid packages, automated ITBs, sub discovery, bid tracking), Bid Leveling (side-by-side sub bid comparison with scope gap detection), Bid Costing (markup, overhead, contingency, bond, AI margin alerts), AI Proposal Generator (federal-compliant drafting, export .doc or PDF), L2/L3 Schedule Generation, and Professional Takeoff Services (expert US estimators at $15/hr as an add-on).

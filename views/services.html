About Takeoff Services

From what takeoffs are to how BidcoreAI delivers them — answered clearly.

Takeoff services involve reviewing construction drawings, plans, and specifications to measure quantities of materials, labor, and scope items required for a project. These quantities form the foundation of accurate estimates, faster bids, and better project planning. Without a solid takeoff, bids are guesswork — with one, every line item is grounded in real measurements.
Accurate takeoffs help contractors reduce costly mistakes, avoid underbidding, improve material planning, control risk, and submit bids faster with greater confidence. Underbidding a job because of a missed quantity is one of the most common and avoidable ways contractors lose money. A proper takeoff eliminates that risk.
Takeoff services are used across the construction industry by General Contractors, Subcontractors, Builders, Developers, Estimators, Project Managers, Renovation Companies, and Preconstruction Teams. Whether you are bidding one project a month or twenty, professional takeoffs help you scale capacity without scaling headcount.
BidcoreAI supports takeoffs across multiple trades including Drywall & Framing, Lumber, Roofing & Siding, Concrete, Flooring, Doors & Windows, Mechanical / Electrical / Plumbing (MEP), Painting, Insulation, and Sitework. If it appears on a set of construction drawings, we can measure it.
Our primary workflow uses the BidcoreAI AI-Intelligent Takeoff Tool, built to improve speed, consistency, and estimating efficiency. For clients who need external file compatibility or prefer a familiar review environment, we can also provide deliverables in alternate formats such as PlanSwift, based on project requirements—making it easy to review, hand off, continue, or verify quantities using a tool you already know.
